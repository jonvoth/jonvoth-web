Travel Plans

We will arrange for your flight, hotel room and transportation to and from the airport. Once we have received your registration information, our travel representative will contact you to plan your air travel. You will fly into the Washington Dulles International airport.

When you arrive at the airport, you will be met at the baggage claim area and taken to the Westfields Marriott.

Travel Packets

Approximately 10 days before you depart, you will receive a travel packet in the mail. It will include your hotel confirmation, tickets (if we arranged your travel) and the flight itinerary information.
