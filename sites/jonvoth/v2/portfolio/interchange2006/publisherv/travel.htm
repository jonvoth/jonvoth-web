Airline and Hotel Information

You are responsible for making your own flight arrangements; however, Navarre will reserve your hotel rooms. Be sure to include the names and travel dates for all the attendees from your company when you register for Interchange. We will also need a credit card number to secure your hotel rooms. If you have questions regarding airline ticketing, please contact Travel and Transport, Marylu Lueke at 1-800-228-4395, email: mlueke@tandt.com.

Transportation to the Hotel

Transportation to and from the hotel is included in your room rate. There will be a shuttle every half hour. Please send your arrival and departure dates and times to Rachael Schmieg at Rachael.Schmieg@navarre.com and she will confirm shuttle times for you.

Lodging

All room rates are $325 per night. This includes tax and transportation to and from the airport. Check-in time is 4:00pm and Check-out is 2:00pm. Once you have finished checking in, pick up your Interchange items at our Hospitality Desk located in the hotel lobby. Publishers will be responsible for basic room charges and for any attendee cancellations occurring after July 10, 2006.

Want to arrange a room drop?

We’ll be happy to coordinate your room drop with the hotel staff. Contact Lisa Carlson-Hammer, Lisa.Hammer@navarre.com or Rachael Schmieg, Rachael.Schmieg@navarre.com with the information and date of the room drop. The charge for room drops is $2 per room.
